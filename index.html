Greece Family Holiday – June 2025

13 Jun – Athens

Arrival, Plaka stroll. Lunch at Kostas Souvlaki, Dinner at To Kafeneio.

14 Jun – Athens

Visit Acropolis, Agora, National Garden, Lycabettus sunset. Lunch: Ta Karamanlidika, Dinner: Klimataria.

15–16 Jun – Aegina

Kolona site, pistachio market, Temple of Aphaia, Agia Marina beach, St Nektarios. Dinner: Remetzo.

17–22 Jun – Poros

Beach, Love Bay, Askeli, Kanali. Optional day trips to Hydra or Moni Island.

23–24 Jun – Nafplio

Explore Old Town, Karathona Beach. Optional trip to Epidaurus.

25–26 Jun – Aegina

Relax before return to Athens.

27 Jun – Fly Out

Departure from Athens.
